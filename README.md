### Voice of the Next Generation

Aawaj is a modern **MERN stack based blog and media platform** focused on Nepali youth, politics, education, technology, and social issues.  
The goal of Aawaj is to provide a space where young people can express opinions, share real stories, and challenge the system through words.

> **Tagline:** *Aba chup basdaina.*
